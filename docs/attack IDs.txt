These values are in bytes, and are used primarily by animation tools.
AI functions will consistently switch the byte values to what is seen below:

0 = no attack (idle, moving, etc)
1 = regular melee
2 = sprint attack
3 = roll attack
4 = summoning
5 = spit attack
6 = melee windup
7 = warning
8 = bite
9 = swipe (WideRangeAttack)
10 = Extreme special (Banishment, Core Eject)
11 = Smite (Magic)
